Chefchaouen city

The Bleu City

Located in Morocco

  • Chefchaouen, also known as the "Blue Pearl of Morocco," is a captivating city nestled in the Rif Mountains of northwest Morocco. Renowned for its strikingly blue-painted buildings that adorn the narrow winding streets, Chefchaouen offers a surreal and picturesque atmosphere.

  • The blue hues are said to have originated from the Jewish population that once resided here, as they believed the color symbolized spiritual significance.

  • Today, the city remains a popular destination for travelers seeking tranquility, cultural immersion, and stunning vistas. With its charming blend of Moroccan
    and Andalusian influences, Chefchaouen enchants visitors with its bustling markets, authentic cuisine, and welcoming locals, making it an unforgettable gem in Morocco's diverse tapestry.
